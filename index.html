About Us

A Legacy of Excellence in Energy Trading

Based in Leiden, The Netherlands — a key location near Europe's energy infrastructure — Faber Oil & Gas B.V. has grown into a key facilitator for the oil and gas sector. Our core focus is the procurement of refined petroleum products from reliable and consistent suppliers for buyers around the globe.

Our experienced team takes the time to understand individual needs, ensuring services are delivered with the best possible solutions. We believe that trust, transparency, and mutual success form the foundation of every successful business relationship.
